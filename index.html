How I Think

Systems first. Details always.

I like understanding how moving parts connect before trying to improve them. Whether I am testing a game, helping a customer, writing a song, or building a concept, I look for patterns, friction points, and the small details that shape the final experience.

  • Understand the system before judging the surface.
  • Find friction before forcing solutions.
  • Balance creativity with practical follow-through.
  • Reflect, improve, and keep building.
